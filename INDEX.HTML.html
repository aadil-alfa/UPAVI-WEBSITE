Who We Are

A Home Away
From Home

UPAVI Malayali Association is the heart of the Keralite community at Central University of Andhra Pradesh. We bring together students from across God's Own Country — united by language, culture, and the shared experience of studying far from home.

From Onam sadhyas to academic seminars, cultural performances to peer support networks — UPAVI is where Kerala's spirit thrives on the red soil of Andhra Pradesh.
